Support

PRSM is a 501(c)(3) non-profit dance organization that provides affordable and accessible events in the North Carolina Triangle Area. The organization believes that a Strong Community builds Stronger Individuals.

Your generous donation will provide financial support for the following:

  • Expenses for hosting dance workshops including but not limited to guest choreographer fees, venue costs, logistics.
  • Transportation and housing for guest choreographers and PRSM_Local events
  • Programming expenses for major events such as PRSM Black Showcase and Workshop.

Your kind support will help alleviate the costs associated to making PRSM's current and future seasons great which in return will continue to foster the growth individuals and communities alike.
